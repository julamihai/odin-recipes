Mamaliga

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of ghee
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 spoon of salt

A little history:

What are the origins and history of the Romanian dish Mamaliga? Mămăligă is a traditional Romanian dish that has been a staple in Romanian and Moldovan cuisine for centuries. It is believed to have originated in the rural areas of Romania and Moldova, where it was a common food for peasants and a substitute for bread.
